## {{ cite "Pokémon" }} 30th Anniversary

The {{ cite "Pokémon" }} franchise was celebrating its 30-year anniversary on February 27, as 27 February 1996 saw the release of {{ cite "Pokémon Red Version" }} and {{ cite "Pokémon Green Version" }}, the very first Pokémon games.

On February 27, I tuned in to watch [Pokémon Presents live on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dh2WcRJOVPU). Among the announcements made during the presentation, {{ cite "Pokémon Pokopia" }}, a new spin-off game, and {{ cite "Pokémon Winds and Waves" }}, the first games of the tenth generation of Pokémon, piqued my interest the most. However, both are exclusive to Nintendo Switch 2, which I do not have yet, and will not be getting any time soon due to it being too expensive to me, and there is no Switch 2-exclusive games that make me actually want to buy the console for yet.

That said, I am very glad that there is finally a Pokémon based on Pomeranian dogs — [Pombon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pombon_(Pok%C3%A9mon)), the Fire-type starter Pokémon in {{ cite "Winds and Waves" }}. [Pomeranian dogs are one of my favourite dog breeds](2025-05-06-i-love-pomeranian-dogs.md), so even though I typically pick the Water-type starter Pokémon when starting to play a main series {{ cite "Pokémon" }} game for the first time, but if I get to play {{ cite "Winds and Waves" }}, I'll make an exception for Pombon.

Naturally, as a [huge fan of {{ cite "Cassette Beasts" }}](2024-11-19-my-ultimate-comfort-media.md), Pombon immediately reminded me of [Pombomb](https://wiki.cassettebeasts.com/wiki/Pombomb), another Fire-type creature based on Pomeranian dogs in a monster collecting game.

Aside from everything that was announced in Pokémon Presents, logos featuring every Pokémon species to commemorate the 30-year anniversary of the franchise were released. The {{ cite "Pokémon" }} fan wiki [Bulbapedia](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Main_Page) has an article for the [Pokémon 30th Anniversary logos](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_30th_Anniversary_logos).

## Games I Was Playing

{{ cite "Scarlet Hollow" }}, an episodic horror visual novel developed by Black Tabby Games, recently released Episode 5, after a hiatus since Episode 4 was released in December 2022 due Black Tabby Games focusing on their other horror visual novel {{ cite "Slay the Princess" }}.

I was immediately hooked again after returning to {{ cite "Scarlet Hollow" }}. There are still two more episodes to be released for the game to be officially completed, but Episode 5 has started to reveal the overarching narrative to the forefront, raising the stakes even higher.

If you are a fan of horror, visual novels, choice-based narrative games, or any combination of the above, I cannot recommend Black Tabby Games' works enough.
